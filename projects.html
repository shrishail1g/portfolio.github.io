My Projects

VerseCraft: AI-Powered Poetry Generator

VerseCraft is a creative AI tool built using a fine-tuned GPT-Neo model trained on a custom poetry dataset. It captures poetic structure, rhythm, and theme to generate original verses with high semantic coherence. In comparative tests, it often outperforms GPT-4 in thematic richness and emotional flow, making it a powerful AI for generative literature.

PrepVerse: Placement Preparation Platform

PrepVerse is a comprehensive placement preparation website designed for students targeting campus placements. It brings together aptitude practice, technical quizzes, and interview tips — all in one clean, accessible space. The platform addresses scattered resources and offers personalized preparation support for a smoother job hunt.

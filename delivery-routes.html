Optimizing Delivery Routes (Using Graph Algorithms)

📌 Business Case

Delivery agents must deliver to multiple locations in one trip — what's the optimal path considering real-time constraints?

🔧 Algorithms

  • Dijkstra’s Algorithm / A*
  • Floyd-Warshall
  • TSP (Traveling Salesman Problem)
  • Minimum Spanning Tree (MST)

🧠 Why it's needed

Avoid fuel waste, reduce delivery time, maximize agent efficiency.

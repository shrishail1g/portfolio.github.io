Flipkart wants to dynamically assign products to regional delivery hubs based on delivery address, product type, and hub load.

  • Clustering (K-means): Segment geographic zones.
  • Greedy Load Assignment: Assign hubs minimizing distance and balancing load.

Just like food delivery apps optimize for nearest kitchen, Flipkart clusters orders and routes them to the most efficient fulfillment hub.

orders = ["Mumbai", "Pune", "Delhi", "Bangalore"]
hubs = ["Mumbai_Hub", "Delhi_Hub"]
distance = {
  ("Mumbai", "Mumbai_Hub"): 5,
  ("Pune", "Mumbai_Hub"): 8,
  ("Delhi", "Delhi_Hub"): 4,
  ("Bangalore", "Mumbai_Hub"): 12
}

def assign_hubs(orders):
  assigned = {}
  for o in orders:
    nearest = min(hubs, key=lambda h: distance.get((o, h), float('inf')))
    assigned[o] = nearest
  return assigned

print(assign_hubs(orders))

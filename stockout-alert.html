Flipkart Business Case

Stockout Prevention Alert

üìå Business Case

Flipkart wants to predict when a product is at risk of running out of stock and alert sellers in advance to replenish inventory.

üß† Algorithms Used

  • Time Series Forecasting (ARIMA / LSTM): Predict future sales volume.
  • Threshold-Based Triggers: Trigger alerts when predicted stock < reorder level.
  • Regression Models: Estimate average daily movement per product-category.

üß† Intuition

Just like a petrol light warns you before running empty, Flipkart predicts low stock events and prompts sellers to refill in time.

üí• Impact

Prevents lost sales due to stockouts, ensures high availability of fast-moving items, improves seller performance metrics.

üíª Sample Base Code (Stock Alert Trigger - Python)

stock_level = {
  "iPhone 13": 20,
  "Redmi Note 12": 4,
  "Samsung M14": 0
}

threshold = 5

def alert(stock_level):
  for product, count in stock_level.items():
    if count < threshold:
      print(f"ALERT: Replenish {product}! Remaining: {count}")

alert(stock_level)
